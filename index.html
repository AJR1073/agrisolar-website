About AgriSolar LLC

Your Trusted Partner in Solar Farm Management

Team Experience

At AgriSolar LLC, while we are newly venturing into solar farm vegetation management, our team brings extensive experience in operating large-scale agricultural machinery, honed over years of managing a 1,000-acre farm. Our expertise in handling substantial farm equipment ensures we can effectively manage the unique challenges of maintaining solar energy facilities.

Additionally, our team holds Commercial Pesticide Licenses, enabling us to apply herbicides safely and in compliance with regulatory standards, which is crucial for effective vegetation control in solar farms.

Mission and Values

As a family-operated business, AgriSolar LLC is dedicated to enhancing the efficiency and sustainability of solar energy facilities through comprehensive vegetation management services. Our mission is to support the renewable energy sector by maintaining optimal site conditions, thereby contributing to the longevity and performance of solar farms.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading provider of solar farm vegetation management services, recognized for our excellence in service, collaborative partnerships, and outstanding performance. We aim to set industry standards by integrating sustainable practices, innovative solutions, and a commitment to environmental stewardship.

Our Services

  • Seeding Native Plants: Establishing native vegetation to promote biodiversity and improve soil health.
  • Erosion Control: Implementing measures to prevent soil erosion, protecting both the environment and infrastructure.
  • Ongoing Maintenance: Regular upkeep including mowing and herbicide application.
